# Future Features (Vision)

This document lists features that are **intentionally out of scope** for the current backend scaffold but are part of the long-term product vision. Use it for roadmap planning and to avoid scope creep in early iterations.

---

## Social Graph / Follows

- User follow/unfollow (followers, following).
- Feed of plans from followed users.
- Privacy controls based on follow relationships.

---

## Reviews & Ratings

- Place reviews (text + optional rating).
- Aggregate ratings per place.
- Moderation and abuse handling.

---

## Media / Gallery

- Photo and video uploads for places and plans.
- CDN or object storage integration.
- Thumbnails and multiple resolutions.

---

## Notifications

- In-app and/or push notifications.
- Triggers: new followers, plan updates, comments, etc.
- Preferences and notification settings.

---

## Payments / Booking

- Integration with payment providers (Stripe, etc.).
- Booking flow for bookable places (restaurants, activities).
- Refunds, cancellations, and receipts.

---

## Other Possible Additions

- **Comments** on plans or places.
- **Favourites / Wishlists** (save places or plans).
- **Sharing** (share plan by link, visibility controls).
- **Search** (full-text, filters, geo search using PostGIS).
- **Admin panel** (verify places, manage users, moderation).
- **Email verification** and password reset.
- **OAuth** (Google, Apple, etc.) in addition to email/password.

---
